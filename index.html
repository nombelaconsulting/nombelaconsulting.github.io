About Us

Nombela Consulting Group is a sustainability, ESG, and governance advisory firm working across Africa and emerging markets.

Founded by a Chartered Accountant (SA), the firm brings together strong financial discipline, governance expertise, and contextual understanding of African development and investment landscapes. Our work focuses on enabling organisations to move beyond ESG compliance toward practical, decision-useful sustainability integration.

We partner with clients that operate in regulated, high-impact, or development-oriented environments and require credible advisory support grounded in both global standards and local realities.

Our Team
