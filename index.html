Who We Are

Nombela Consulting Group is an Africa-based sustainability, ESG, and governance advisory firm working across the continent and selected emerging markets.

Founded by a Chartered Accountant (SA), the firm brings together strong financial discipline, governance expertise, and contextual understanding of African development and investment landscapes.

Our work focuses on helping organisations move beyond ESG compliance toward practical, decision-useful sustainability integration that supports long-term value creation, institutional credibility, and responsible capital deployment.

We partner with public institutions, development finance actors, investors, and private-sector organisations operating in regulated, high-impact, or development-oriented environments that require credible, senior-level advisory support.

Leadership

Ndumiso Ngwabi – Founder & Managing Director

Ndumiso is a Chartered Accountant (SA) and sustainability and governance advisor with experience spanning professional services, financial services, and impact-oriented advisory environments across Africa.

He has worked on engagements involving ESG strategy, governance reviews, impact-oriented due diligence, and non-financial reporting, supporting institutions and organisations navigating complex regulatory and stakeholder landscapes.

His work is grounded in the integration of governance, financial integrity, and sustainability — enabling organisations to pursue their mandates without compromising accountability, credibility, or long-term resilience.
