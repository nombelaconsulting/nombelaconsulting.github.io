Value Proposition

Helping mission-driven organisations move from vision to execution.

Nombela Consulting Group supports social enterprises, nonprofits, founders, and institutional partners working at the intersection of development, governance, and systems change.

Our work spans strategic partnerships, institutional structuring, fundraising support, governance design, philanthropy advisory, and operational strategy — helping initiatives build credibility, attract support, and execute effectively in complex environments.

Operating under Nombela Group (Pty) Ltd (established 2020), the firm combines financial rigour with deep understanding of community-rooted impact work. Founded by a Chartered Accountant with international experience across London, New York, and Johannesburg, Nombela Consulting Group bridges institutional strategy with practical execution across emerging markets.
