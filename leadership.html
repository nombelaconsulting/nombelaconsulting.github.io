Founder Profile

A strategist working at the intersection of finance, governance, philanthropy, and community-driven systems change.

With professional experience spanning London, New York, and Johannesburg, Ndumiso brings institutional-level financial and governance expertise into mission-driven work across Africa.

His work increasingly focuses on helping organisations build credible structures, strategic partnerships, and scalable systems capable of delivering meaningful social impact.

"The most durable change happens when communities are part of the architecture — not just the beneficiaries."

As Director of the One K Foundation, he designs impact metrics and financial sustainability strategies for public health initiatives, bridging the gap between capital and social outcomes.

Social Impact Work

One K Foundation

Beyond advisory practice, Ndumiso serves as a Director of the One K Foundation — bridging capital and social outcomes in public health and community development.

This work informs and deepens the Nombela Consulting Group's approach: bringing real-world experience of what it means to design systems that must actually work for communities — not just satisfy institutional requirements.

The Foundation work also grounds the firm's philanthropic advisory practice, with direct experience of funder relationships, governance design, and impact measurement in practice.
